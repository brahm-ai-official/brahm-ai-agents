# BRAHM AI Agents Roadmap

This roadmap explains the current direction of BRAHM AI Agents as a multilingual AI phone agent platform.

## Phase 1: Stability and Runtime Quality

Current focus:

- Improve AI phone call quality
- Reduce first-response latency
- Improve multilingual reply accuracy
- Strengthen agent role-based replies
- Improve lead capture behavior
- Improve conversation memory
- Improve call handling reliability
- Improve dashboard visibility

## Phase 2: Business Dashboard and CRM Workflows

Planned improvements:

- Better lead management
- Lead stages
- Follow-up status tracking
- Notes and timeline view
- Conversation filtering
- Call history improvements
- Business profile management
- Agent performance overview
- Usage tracking
- Team-ready dashboard structure

## Phase 3: Multilingual Voice Expansion

Planned improvements:

- More language support
- Better Indian language handling
- Improved voice quality
- Faster greeting response
- Smarter fallback handling
- Natural conversation flow
- Better interruption and pause handling
- Improved pronunciation and expression

## Phase 4: Billing and Subscription Readiness

Planned improvements:

- Subscription plans
- Trial flow
- Usage-based limits
- Billing dashboard
- Provider-based payment support
- Invoice and account management
- Plan-based feature access

## Phase 5: Integrations

Planned integrations:

- Telephony providers
- CRM systems
- Webhooks
- Payment providers
- Business tools
- Lead export
- Third-party automation platforms

## Phase 6: Advanced Agent Intelligence

Future direction:

- Smarter lead qualification
- Better customer intent detection
- Memory-based conversations
- Business-specific agent behavior
- Agent analytics
- Campaign intelligence
- Human handoff support
- Industry-specific agent templates

## Long-Term Vision

BRAHM AI Agents aims to become a practical AI communication platform where businesses 
can create smart multilingual phone agents for real customer conversations, 
lead generation, support, sales and follow-ups.
